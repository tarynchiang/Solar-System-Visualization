Solar System Visualization

Experience a stunning visual representation of the solar system with a focus on the Sun, Earth, and Moon.
This static display uses advanced graphical techniques to create a lifelike depiction of these celestial bodies in space.

Key Features:

  • Advanced Celestial Mechanics: Real-time simulation of solar, lunar, and terrestrial dynamics, accurately depicting their relative positions and interactions.
  • Detailed Surface Rendering: Utilizes high-resolution textures and procedural turbulence to render realistic surfaces for the Sun, Earth, and Moon.
  • Dynamic Lighting and Shadows: Implements complex lighting models, including Phong shading and ambient, diffuse, and specular components, to mimic natural lighting conditions in space.
  • Ray-Sphere Intersection: Efficient calculations for ray tracing, detecting intersections with celestial bodies to render views from any perspective.
  • Atmospheric and Phenomenal Effects: Simulates atmospheric scattering on Earth and dynamic corona effects around the Sun, enhancing the visual realism of sunrise and sunset transitions.
